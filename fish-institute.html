FISH INSTITUTE

FISH Institute provides academic and cultural integration support for international Chinese students beginning their studies in the United States. Providing essential resources for internationals on an academic and social level, FISH Institute is leading a groundbreaking integration program at the university level.

PROJECTS

FISH Institute was seeking to build complete concept visuals for investor presentations as well as build a brand which has its roots dug deep in Chinese culture. It would prove essential to build a brand with cultural ties, helping FISH Institute in their pre-angel round of funding. This proof-of-concept required a full-functioning brand within a website environment, and positioning to support a mobile application in the future.

RESPONSIBILITES

Collaboration between the co-founders and collecting their feedback was a essential to this project. Outlining the primary goal of the product, in a clean and presentable UI would have preliminary impact on investors as well as play a piece in the long-term success of the company. To get FISH Institute off the ground and running, their brand identity needed to be well-thought and properly executed. In addition, building a basic UI highlighting various key elements remained an invaluable component of the build.
