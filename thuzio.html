PROJECTS

My role at Thuzio vastly grew from my initial entry point on the team in May 2014. Originally a Marketing Designer, I quickly transitioned to assisting the Senior Product Designer on the UI Development of our two products. In June '15 I transitioned full-time to Product focusing solely on UI/UX Design.


During the rebrand of Thuzio 360, I remained heavily involved in the ideation and creation of the new brand, the mark and associated colors. Ensuring the new color scheme would compliment the UI, as well as support marketing and their effots when building new collateral assets.


RESPONSIBILITES

Working to develop our UI/UX of our two platforms, I focus on the prompt redevelopment and improvement of various features of both webapps. In addition, partner with Software Engineers and Front-End Developers to ensure specs are properly met, designs executed properly.

Through our agile environment, efficiency is a must and having only one dedicated Product Manager on the team has allowed me to fill shoes whenever possible. Taking on basic PM roles have allowed me to understand and grasp the product development cycle and to think outside the box.
