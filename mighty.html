MIGHTY

Mighty is the first plaintiff-funding SaaS product, providing legal and medical funders, and their organizations, an end-to-end platform to continue empowering plaintiffs to get a better deal from the legal system. The product allows funders the ability to sort and monitor key cases, update various pieces of case information, as well as generate contracts, input returns, send emails and more.

RESPONSIBILITES

As a UX/UI Designer at Mighty, I was responsible for the implementation of user flows, experiential design, as well as development of the user interface design of new features. Additionally, I worked alongside software engineers and front-end developers to ensure mockups were delivered pixel perfect, working to resolve any issues faced during the process.


Much of the process was guided by user-data from the platform, delivered via various methods. Some methods included website analytics and customer calls, while occasionally changes were made without advanced consultation and observation and minor outreach was necessary to uncover user-reactions.
