PROJECTS

The Pirate Blue Athletic Fund meets a rigorous schedule for events so deadlines are much closer than other projects. With many events necessitating custom invitations, designing for print makes for a great challenge. Creating uniform and consistent pieces allows for a cohesive pattern for an academic year.


Projects have included the implementation of a mobile landing page to facilitate the growth of the Pirate Pride Challenge, Hall of Fame graphics and invitations, various flyers and digital assets to support their fundraising events.

RESPONSIBILITES

Developing unique and identifiable styles for each respective project and maintaining the brand of Seton Hall Athletics was a primary task. Communicating with project managers and other senior-level staff became essential for proper delivery of all desired assets. In addition, this role requires basic front-end development to build sites which communicate new campaigns beyond the standard print channels.
